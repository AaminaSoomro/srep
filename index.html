Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine shredded zucchini, corn, green onions, chickpea flour, garlic, cumin, oregano, thyme, salt & pepper.
  2. Stir together until well combined.
  3. Allow to sit for at least 5 minutes. This step is important because the flour will absorb the moisture from the zucchini and create a batter.
  4. Warm a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Grease with preferred cooking oil then scoop out 1/4 cup of the zucchini mixture at a time into the skillet.
  5. Cook for about 3-5 minutes on each side, until light golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with black beans, salsa, nacho cashew cream and/or avocado then enjoy!
